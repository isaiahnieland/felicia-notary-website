About

I’m a commissioned notary serving Clay County, Missouri. I started this service to help the community with important paperwork, reduce fraud through proper notarization, and offer a convenient option for people who can’t easily get to a bank or office.

I meet you at a neutral, comfortable location (library, coffee shop, or your office) and make sure you understand what we’re signing. I can also print and mail documents on your behalf when needed.

This site was built and is hosted with help from my son.

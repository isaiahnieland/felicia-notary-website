Services

I offer the following notary services in Clay County, Missouri. We meet at a neutral location (library, coffee shop) for retail clients, or at your office or facility for commercial clients. I do not do home visits.

What I offer

  • General notarization — Signatures, acknowledgments, oaths. Standard documents and forms.
  • Loan signings — Notarization for loan documents. (Additional training may apply; ask when booking.)
  • Print and mail — I can print documents and mail them on your behalf for a fee (see Pricing).
  • Fingerprinting — Available upon request.
  • Medallion signatures — Available for qualifying documents.

Service area & availability

Area: Clay County, Missouri only.

Availability: Weekends all day; weekdays after 5:00 PM. Book a time that works for you — I’ll confirm the exact meeting location (library, coffee shop, or your office) after you book.

Service details

Individual services and what they include. Need something that isn’t in a package? We can do à la carte too.

Service Description
General notarization Signatures, acknowledgments, and oaths for standard documents and forms. I’ll meet you at a neutral location and notarize on the spot.
Loan signings Notarization for loan documents, including refinances and real estate. I travel to you and handle the full signing; additional training may apply — ask when booking.
Print & mail I print your documents and mail them on your behalf. Fee covers printing and postage; see Pricing for details.
Fingerprinting Fingerprinting services available upon request. Contact me when booking to confirm availability.
Medallion signatures Medallion signature guarantees for qualifying documents (e.g. certain financial forms). Ask when you book.
